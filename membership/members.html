Members

The Devonport Yacht Club (Inc) offers members a unique opportunity to belong to a club that has an enormous historical background steeped in the sailing history of New Zealand, with a clubhouse that boasts one of the finest views of the Waitemata Harbour.

It provides a range of facilities for it's members, including Racing, Family Cruising, Maintenance Facilities and Superb Dining on Friday nights. The Dinner Service time is 1800 hours to 2000 hours (6.00pm to 8.00pm).

The bar is open at the following times, so that you can relax and enjoy the view, and watch the sunset over Auckland City.

Thursday 4.30pm to 8.30pm
Friday 4.30 till late
Saturday 4.30 to 8.30pm
Sunday 4.30pm to 8.30pm

These times are indicative and will depend on patronage on the night.

A range of events are planned over the coming season.

The Clubhouse is available for Private Functions. The costs for the use of the clubhouse for functions are available from the Function Co-ordinator.

All members of overseas Yacht Clubs, and visiting guests are more than welcome to visit the club, and enjoy its superb location, and facilities.

Membership of the club is open to all with an interest in Sailing. An active Associate Membership complements the Family and Senior membership groups.

There are other categories of membership. Full details on all aspects of membership can be found in the Rules but they are summarised here.

The Family Membership entitles the member, partner and any children under 18 years to full membership and all club privileges such as haul-out, racing , clubhouse use, hire of dinghy lockers etc.

The Ordinary Membership is a single full membership (ordinary member) with all the club privileges as above.

Associate Membership is restricted to non boat owners and has clubhouse use privileges only.

There is no joining fee and the annual subscriptions from July 2015 are as follows:

Family Member $275.00
Ordinary Member $240.00
Associate Member $130.00
Family Associate Member  $150.00
Junior Member  $25.00
Country Member   $125.00
Veteran Family Member  $147.00
Veteran Ordinary Member  $125.00
Veteran Country Member  $75.00

Full details on each of these Membership categories can be found in the Club Rules

New members joining will be charged a proportion of the annual fee for the balance of the membership year remaining. Membership application forms are available from the foyer of the clubhouse or download and complete the application form from the link below:

Membership Application Form (Acrobat reader required)
